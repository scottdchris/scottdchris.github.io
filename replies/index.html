The new way to get more views on your social media content.

click·bait

/ˈklikbāt/

noun informal

(on the Internet) content, especially that of a sensational or provocative nature, whose main purpose is to attract attention and draw visitors to a particular web page.


Now-a-days, users are starting to shy away from these headlines due to the often unfulfilling nature of content beyond the link.
